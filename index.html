How to play

Step 1

Each player gets 7 cards.

Step 2

Match by rank or faction.

Step 3

Use special cards to steal, skip, reverse, chain, and change faction.

Step 4

First player with no cards wins immediately.

Critical rule: Say LAST when you have 1 card, or draw a penalty.

Special cards

7 Chain Punishment

Next player draws 2 or plays another 7. Every extra 7 adds +2 cards to the penalty.

2 Forced Theft

Choose a player to take one random card from your hand face-down.

E Combo Card

Play another E, match faction, or draw 1 and end turn.

L Change Faction

Change the active faction to any faction in the current game mode.

H Skip Turn

The next player loses their turn.

V Reverse Direction

Direction flips instantly and changes the pressure at the table.
